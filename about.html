🎯 Our Mission

Whether you're a casual player or a hardcore enthusiast, we provide the tools to organize your collection, discover new adventures, and connect with a community that shares your passion.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is GameShelf free to use?

Yes! GameShelf is completely free. We believe in making game tracking accessible to everyone.

Do I need to create an account?

Currently, GameShelf uses local storage to save your data. No account is needed, but your data is stored on your device.

Where does the game data come from?

We use the RAWG Video Games Database API, which provides information on over 500,000 games across all platforms.

Can I access my library on multiple devices?

Currently, data is stored locally on your device. We're working on cloud sync features for future updates.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Head over to our Contact page and send us a message. We love hearing from our users!
